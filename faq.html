Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before commissioning. Can't find it? Just ask the bot or email me.

Ordering
How do I place a commission?+
Email errorarmy987@gmail.com with your idea, reference images, and chosen package. I'll confirm availability and send payment details. Work starts after advance payment is received.
When does work begin?+
Work begins after advance payment is confirmed. You'll be added to the queue and I'll start in order.
What payment methods do you accept?+
UPI is the primary method. Other options can be arranged — mention your preferred method when you reach out.
Can I request something custom?+
Absolutely! If your idea doesn't fit a listed package, describe it in your email. Custom pricing is available for unique requests.
Do you take rush orders?+
Rush delivery is available on Elite-tier with priority slots. Mention your deadline and I'll confirm feasibility.
Artwork & Process
How many revisions do I get?+
Starter gets 1 revision, Pro gets 2, Elite gets unlimited. Minor tweaks (colour, small edits) are always free. Major changes count as a revision round.
How long does delivery take?+
Anime PFPs: 2–4 days · OC Designs: 4–7 days · Animations: 7–14 days. Times may vary during busy periods — I'll give you an estimate upfront.
What file formats will I receive?+
Final files are high-res PNG (transparent BG available on request). Animations delivered as MP4 or GIF. Source files are not included.
Can I use the art commercially?+
All packages include full personal use rights. For commercial use (merch, branding etc.), a commercial licence fee applies. Contact me before ordering for rates.
Will my art be shared publicly?+
Completed commissions may be shared on portfolio and socials as examples. If you'd like your piece kept private, just mention it when ordering.

Still have a question?

I reply within 24 hours — reach out directly or use the chat bot.
