Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before commissioning. Can't find the answer? Just ask.

Ordering
01
How do I place a commission? +
Email errorarmy987@gmail.com with your idea, reference images, and your chosen package. I'll confirm availability and send payment details. Work starts after advance payment is received.
When does work actually begin? +
Work begins after the advance payment is confirmed. Once paid, you'll be added to the queue and I'll start on your commission in order.
What payment methods are accepted? +
UPI is the primary method. Other options can be arranged — mention your preferred method when you reach out and we'll sort it out.
Can I request something not listed? +
Absolutely! If your idea doesn't fit neatly into the listed packages, just describe it in your email. Custom pricing is available for unique requests.
Do you take rush orders? +
Rush delivery is available on Elite-tier commissions with priority slots. Mention your deadline in the email and I'll let you know if it's feasible.
Artwork & Process
02
How many revisions do I get? +
Starter gets 1 revision, Pro gets 2, and Elite gets unlimited revisions. Minor tweaks (colour, small edits) are always free. Major changes like full redraws count as a revision round.
How long does delivery take? +
Anime PFPs: 2–4 days · OC Designs: 4–7 days · Animations: 7–14 days. Times may vary during busy periods — I'll always give you an estimate upfront.
What file formats will I receive? +
Final files are delivered as high-resolution PNG (transparent BG available on request). Animations are delivered as MP4 or GIF. Source files are not included.
Can I use the art commercially? +
All packages include full personal use rights. For commercial use (merch, branding, etc.), a commercial licence fee applies. Contact me for rates before placing a commercial order.
Will my art be posted publicly? +
Completed commissions may be shared on the portfolio and socials as examples of my work. If you'd like your commission kept private, just mention it when ordering and I'll respect that.

Still have a question?

I reply within 24 hours — just reach out directly.
